Years of Heritage

Ethiopia is the birthplace of Arabica coffee — the land where the plant first grew wild and where the ritual of the cup began. From these highlands, Eko Cup Trading brings the country's finest single-origin expressions to roasters across the world, with the traceability and care that specialty coffee demands.

Our Story

Coffee Was Born Here.
We Carry It Forward.

Long before coffee reached the world's cups, it grew wild in the forests of Ethiopia. That heritage isn't a tagline for us — it's the ground we stand on.

Eko Cup Trading is an Addis Ababa–based exporter of single-origin Ethiopian specialty green coffee. We work at the source, alongside the smallholder farmers and washing stations of Yirgacheffe, Sidama, and Guji, to bring fully traceable lots to roasters around the world.

Ethiopia's highlands give coffee something no other origin can replicate: thousands of indigenous heirloom varieties, elevations climbing past 2,000 metres, and generations of growers who know the land intimately. Our work is to protect that character at every step — from selective cherry picking and careful fermentation to raised-bed drying and repeated cupping — so that what leaves our mill is exactly what the farm intended.

We believe direct relationships make better coffee and fairer trade. By removing unnecessary intermediaries, we return more value to the farming communities who make this work possible, and we give our partners abroad consistent quality, dependable supply, and a name they can trust season after season.

The Process

A disciplined chain of decisions, at origin, on the bed, and at the cupping table, protects the clarity that defines great Ethiopian coffee.

01

Direct Sourcing

We work directly with smallholder farmers across Ethiopia's highlands, providing fair prices and technical support for better yields.

02

Meticulous Processing

Our washed lots go through careful cherry selection, fermentation monitoring, and raised-bed drying to achieve clarity and complexity.

03

Quality Control

Every lot is cupped multiple times at our Addis Ababa lab before export. We only ship what meets our 84+ SCA score threshold.

Our Farms

Rooted in Ethiopia's Highlands

Our farms and partner washing stations span three of Ethiopia's most celebrated coffee-growing regions — each with distinct terroir, microclimates, and flavour identities that have captivated roasters for generations. Across these highlands, cool nights, mineral-rich soils, and elevations above 2,000 metres slow the cherries as they ripen, concentrating the sweetness and delicate aromatics that make Ethiopian coffee unmistakable in the cup.
